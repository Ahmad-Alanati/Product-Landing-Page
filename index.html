overview

"The Last of Us" is a video game developed by Naughty Dog and released in 2013. It is a post-apocalyptic action-adventure game set in the United States in the year 2033, 20 years after a fungal infection has decimated much of the world's population, turning them into aggressive and dangerous creatures known as "infected".

about the game

The game follows the story of Joel, a smuggler who is tasked with escorting a young girl named Ellie across the country in the hope of finding a cure for the infection. The gameplay involves a mix of stealth, exploration, and combat, as Joel and Ellie face various obstacles and dangers on their journey.

players thoughts about the game

The game received critical acclaim for its storytelling, character development, and gameplay mechanics. It was praised for its emotional depth and exploration of themes such as love, loss, and survival in a world that has been destroyed by a deadly infection.
